About Me

I am pursuing research work as an Interdisciplinary Direct PhD scholar in Indian Institute of Technology Madras. I work with Prof. V Kamakoti from Department of Computer Science & Engineering and Dr. Nitin Chandrachoodan from Department of Electrical Engineering.

My research interests include 'Network Security & Privacy' and 'Secure Internet Architecture'. I am also interested in emerging technologies in this domain and have worked on developing secure network solutions based on the Blockchain platform.
